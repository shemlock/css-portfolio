About
Me

I’m Sarah, a UI/UX designer and front-end developer. I like to make fun, interactive things with code! Especially when these things involve working with others to make an even bigger impact. I’m a keen advocate for tech diversity and Women in STEM. Having graduated with a BA in Business Management and Finance, an MSc in Marketing and an MSc in Computer Science, I bring together business acumen, technology innovation, and marketing finesse to create meaningful digital solutions that drive business strategy.

The websites I build are fast, scaleable and bespoke to each client. I’m fiercely dedicated to designing impactful websites that not only look beautiful but leave your competitors in the dust and help your business grow. I like to work in a very collaborative and open way, you will always have a say in what’s going on and I will always give you my honest opinion on what I think will benefit your business the most. If you have an upcoming project you'd like to discuss then please get in touch.
